The brewing story

In 2020, during the unending quarantine, Bidisha Ghosh had an idea while reading Maya Angelou's I Know Why The Caged Bird Sings in her English online class and started reminiscing about having coffee with friends and talking with them about the things they're the most passionate about. Katyayni and Bidisha have been bookworms for as long as they can remember and so they decided to save up all they earned from working tirelessly and opened up their dream of a cafe library. They realized that the immersive culture around coffee in Delhi stood in stark contrast to the transactional nature of big chain coffee places that feel pretentious and classist. The communal vibe and celebratory nature of coffee culture were something that inspired them to share this experience with others. So they did.
